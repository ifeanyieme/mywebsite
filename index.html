Overview

Purpose

Khiddle's Networks is a professional portfolio website designed to showcase my skills, expertise, and achievements in the field of technology.

Our primary audience includes potential employers and business owners seeking to collaborate with a knowledgeable and dedicated tech professional.

The primary objective of this website is to provide a comprehensive overview of my work, projects, and capabilities, allowing potential employers and

clients to make informed decisions about hiring me for their technical needs.

Khiddle's networks aims to foster connections and establish a network of employable individuals who recognize the value of my skills

and are interested in partnering with me for tech-related projects and opportunities."

Audience

Primary Audience:

Employers: My primary audience includes potential employers from technology companies, startups, or businesses looking to hire skilled tech professionals.

These employers are interested in assessing my qualifications, experience, and expertise in technology to determine if i am a suitable candidate for their organization.

Business Owners: Small business owners and entrepreneurs who require technical services or expertise for their projects. They are seeking a reliable tech

professional who can help them implement and manage technology solutions that drive their business forward.

HR Managers: Human resources managers and recruitment professionals responsible for identifying and hiring tech talent within their organizations.

My portfolio website helps them evaluate my qualifications and determine if you meet their hiring criteria.

Secondary Audience:

Fellow Tech Professionals: Other professionals in the tech industry,

including developers, designers, and IT specialists, who may be interested in collaborating or networking with me on various projects.

My portfolio can serve as a point of reference for potential partnerships or collaborations.

Students and Aspiring Tech Professionals:

Individuals pursuing a career in technology or looking for guidance on how to build a successful tech career.

My portfolio can inspire and provide insights into the path to success in the tech industry.

Tech Enthusiasts:

People with a general interest in technology who may visit my website to learn about the latest trends,

innovations, or to gain insights into the tech world.
